Step 1

Think of a question. Some examples:

  • What's the sum of the populations of all cities in China?
  • How many cities have populations over 100,000 and are above 1,000 meters above sea level?
  • What is the population of the largest city in each time zone?
  • Find all the cities in the United States with populations over 1,000,000
